Jack Lowery

WRITER AND TEACHER

My writing has appeared in The Atlantic, The Times Literary Supplement and The Awl. I’m currently working on a book about Gran Fury, a collective of artists associated with ACT UP. (The AIDS Coalition to Unleash Power). I also teach in the Undergraduate Writing Program at Columbia University. If you want to hear my voice, you can listen to my interview with Kai Ryssdal for NPR’s Marketplace.
